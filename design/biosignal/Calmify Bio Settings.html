The framing

This is the only screen in the bio integration that has any "controls" in the traditional sense — every other surface (journal banner, medi card, home narrative, insight card) is read-only context. Settings is where the sovereignty contract lives, and it has to feel like a contract: clear, granular, undramatic.

The order

Master → permissions → sources → privacy → surfaces → tier. We go from "is this even on?" outward — broadest control first, narrowest last. The Pro card sits at the bottom because anyone who scrolled this far is engaged enough to consider it; we don't lead with the upsell.

Try it

Flip the master toggle — the body dims, the connected status collapses, and a reactivate CTA replaces it. Press the destructive row to see the bottom-sheet confirm. Expand the cloud-upload tradeoff for the full privacy ↔ quality explanation. Toggle Light in the gallery header for the light theme.

What's explicitly not here

No "Goals" subsection. No coaching toggles. No "always on" health permissions buried behind a chevron. Each signal has its own on/off and its own delete; each surface where data appears can be silenced. Nothing is mandatory once the master is on.

Delete all bio data?

This removes every reading Calmify has stored — locally and on our servers — across all seven signals. Your narratives will stop until new data arrives. This cannot be undone.
